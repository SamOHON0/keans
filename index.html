Background

Dr Gerald Kean is the Principal of Keans Solicitors, 4/5 Trinity Street, Dublin 2. Born in 1957, he was educated in Colaiste Chriost Ri, Cork, and De La Salle in Wicklow.

He qualified as a Solicitor in 1980, apprenticed under the late Michael Martin Solicitor in Capel Street. He obtained a Bachelor of Civil Law Degree from UCD and an Honorary Doctorate from the American University in Florida.

The Practice

In the late 1980s, Dr Kean opened his practice on Exchequer Street, later moving to Pembroke Street in the early 1990s. The firm has steadily grown and expanded over the years. His main area of focus is Personal Injury Claims.

He specialises in representing a wide variety of clients with a wide variety of legal matters. The firm prides itself on being transparent, open, efficient and in constant communication with clients.

High-Profile Representation

Over the years, Gerald has been involved in acting for national and international artists, premiership league footballers, politicians, and media personnel. Despite this high-profile client base, the firm maintains its commitment to providing excellent legal services to every client who walks through the door.
